home

the end of tomorrows



i wish today would last forever


i wish the night would grow longer and longer,


until the sun forgot to dawn


i’d sink into its inky shadows

 

and at once feel at peace


the stars would envelop me in their warmth

 

and the clouds would muffle all the noise


the moon would whisper in my ear,


“you don’t have to worry anymore”


and i wouldn’t, not a second longer.
